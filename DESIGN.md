# HEALIO MEDIKA — Design Brief

## Purpose & Direction
Trusted healthcare marketplace connecting patients with medical professionals. Professional, warm, compassionate design optimized for elderly users (large fonts, high contrast, clear navigation).

## Visual Tone
Warm professionalism — trustworthy medical aesthetic combined with human care. Green (healthcare trust) + gold (warmth, optimism). Never clinical or cold.

## Color Palette
| Role | OKLCH Light | OKLCH Dark | Usage |
|------|-------------|-----------|-------|
| Primary (Green) | 0.52 0.12 160 | 0.65 0.14 165 | Buttons, links, primary actions, branding |
| Secondary (Gold) | 0.72 0.15 70 | 0.78 0.16 68 | Accents, highlights, CTA emphasis |
| Destructive (Red) | 0.55 0.22 25 | 0.65 0.19 22 | Errors, warnings, cancellations |
| Background | 0.99 0 0 | 0.15 0 0 | Page background |
| Foreground | 0.18 0 0 | 0.96 0 0 | Text |

## Typography
| Tier | Font | Size | Usage |
|------|------|------|-------|
| Display | Fraunces 600 | 32–40px | Page titles, hero sections |
| Body | General Sans 400 | 16–18px | Body text, form labels |
| Mono | System | 13px | Code, technical text |

## Structural Zones
- **Header**: White card (bg-card) with green bottom border, logo, navigation. Elevated shadow.
- **Sidebar**: Light grey (bg-sidebar), green accent on active item.
- **Cards**: White (bg-card) with subtle shadow (shadow-sm), green left border on action cards.
- **CTAs**: Gold buttons (bg-secondary) with primary-foreground text, large padding (px-6 py-3).
- **Forms**: White inputs (bg-input) with green ring on focus, full-width on mobile.
- **Footer**: Light grey (bg-muted/20) with border-t.

## Spacing & Rhythm
Generous padding (1rem–2rem) for easy interaction. 8px base grid. Touch targets ≥44px (elderly accessibility).

## Component Patterns
- Service cards: Image thumbnail, title, description, green "Book" button.
- Status badges: Green (confirmed), gold (pending), red (cancelled).
- Modals: Center, white background, green confirm button, grey dismiss.
- Navigation: Large menu items, hover green underline.

## Motion
Smooth transitions (0.3s cubic-bezier). Fade in on load. No animations that distract or startle.

## Accessibility
High contrast ratio ≥4.5:1. Large touch targets. Clear focus states (green ring). Keyboard navigation supported.

## Signature Detail
Green + gold gradient accent stripe on top of service cards — reinforces brand identity and medical trust with warmth.
